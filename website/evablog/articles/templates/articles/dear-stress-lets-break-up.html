It may sound obvious, but yes: we all stress out. Everyday, about some minor and major issues. Sometimes it’s worth it – sometimes not…So, the thing is – how to actually stop it or, at least, reduce its appearence in your daily routine? Frankly speaking, I have no idea how to remove it from our lives unless you live in a cage far away from civilazation with no internet and mass-media…wait, then you gonna be stressed out about it, aren’t you?) So, here’s a couple of tips I personally use on a weekly bases.

1. First things first, I ALWAYS have an evening that is completely dedicated to myself. Usually it’s a Saturday night: you are already a bit relaxed by that time and you don’t have to hurry anywhere tomorow morning – sounds perfect) So, what can you do? Take a bubble bath, apply some face mask, burn a nice-smelling candle and read a book or listen to music. Sounds strange, but for me it’s important to switch of my laptop and internet. Only this way it feels like relaxation.

Work Out

2. If your mind is bogling with ideas: bright – write them down not to forget, not so bright – work out. Working out really helps to take your mind off some things that bother you. I prefer swimming – ideal or HIIT – when I don’t have much time (P.S. After this kinf of work out I have not just any random thoughts – I have no thoughts at all, except for: can I kill the person who created this work out.

Drink Water

3. Drink water. Sounds cliche. I know. But it is so freaking important. I noticed that when I drink enough water (for me 2,5L) I feel more energised, less tired and fatigued. So, let’s flush this stress away from our body!
